Lasagna

Lasagne bolognese is a delicious pasta in sheets layered with meat stuffing with tomatoes and béchamel sauce. The dish is filling and stands out for its unique flavor. It is certainly an original proposal for dinner with pasta in the leading role. It comes from Italian cuisine and will satisfy even the biggest gourmet, and children also love it.We give you a reliable recipe for lasagna with béchamel sauce.

  • milk 750ml
  • butter 30g
  • flour 45g
  • pinch of salt
  • pinch of pepper
  • pinch of oregano
  • lasagne 20pcs.
  • pork minced meat 500g
  • minced beef
  • garlic 3 cloves
  • onion 2pcs.
  • tomato 500g
  • basil 30g
  • cream cheese 150g
  • tomato concentrate 45g

How to do?

  1. Bechamel sauce: pour milk into a saucepan, add flour, butter, oregano, salt and pepper to taste. Cook everything together for a while, stirring all the time until a thick sauce is formed.
  2. Sos bolognese: mięso wołowe i wieprzowe wrzucamy na patelnię i podsmażamy do odparowania wody. Dodajemy obrane i pokrojone drobno czosnek i cebulę. Pomidory przelewamy gorącą wodą, obieramy je ze skórki, podsmażamy na patelni razem z bazylią, pieprzem i solą. Wszystko razem mieszamy z wcześniej podsmażonym mięsem. Piekarnik nagrzewamy do 200 stopni.
  3. Layering: grease a baking sheet preferably square (medium size) with butter and béchamel sauce. Then lay the pasta sheets (they can overlap a bit), put the bolognese sauce on them, béchamel, sprinkle with yellow cheese. The whole process is repeated 3-4, until the ingredients are finished, finishing with a layer of cheese. Lasagne bolognese bake for about 50 minutes.
